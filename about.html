Bitcoin is not controlled by any government. Anyone in the world with internet access can create a wallet. No bank or company can monitor your balance or what you buy.

Bitcoin is financial freedom.

With financial freedom comes responsibility. No Third Party is your guide to the new world of finance where you, and only you, control your money. In the new world of finance, security and privacy is your responsibility.

Take control of your money and download a bitcoin wallet. It’s the first step towards financial freedom. No Third Party’s wallet overviews help you secure your funds and protect your privacy. The NTP knowledge base is home to detailed tutorials and how-to's that help you take full advantage of Bitcoin and Bitcoin wallets.

We’re here to guide you in this new world of money.

No Third Party was founded by Jordan Tuwiner in November 2014.
