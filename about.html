Your money no longer needs to be stored at a bank. It can be stored in your brain, on a piece of paper, or on a flash drive. Financial freedom comes with responsibility, and it's becoming clearer each and everyday why the world needs Bitcoin. Take control of your wealth so you don't get Greece'd, Cyprus'd, or Goxed.

No Third Party is your guide to the global and frictionless peer-to-peer economy enabled by Bitcoin. We help you take control of your money.

In the new world of finance, security and privacy is your responsibility. Our wallet overviews help you secure your funds and protect your privacy.

The NTP knowledge base is home to detailed tutorials and how-to's that help you take full advantage of Bitcoin and Bitcoin wallets.
